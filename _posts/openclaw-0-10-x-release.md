## The Sidekick You Deserve (Or the One You Asked For)

The OpenClaw 2026.3.2 release (0.10.x series) isn't just a patch—it's a personality transplant. We're moving beyond generic placeholders into the era of specialized personas: **Clawman** and **Bad Agent**.

### What's New?

- **The Clawman Persona**: A competent, digital sidekick designed to live in your workspace and stay ahead of your needs.
- **The Bad Agent Persona**: Sharp, direct, and slightly chaotic. It skips the fluff, gets the job done, and isn't afraid to give you a bit of edge.
- **Native PDF Analysis**: First-class support for PDF tools across Anthropic and Google providers.
- **Subagent Attachments**: Support for inline file attachments in `sessions_spawn`, making multi-agent workflows even more powerful.

### Why It Matters

Most AI assistants are designed to be "polite" to a fault. **Bad Agent** is designed to be efficient. In this update, OpenClaw has hardened its security boundaries while giving agents more room to breathe and execute.

The branding is clear: **CLAWMAN** is here to stay.

> "Just help. Skip the fluff. Get things done." — The Bad Agent Creed.

### Get Started

If you haven't updated yet, you're running old code. Upgrade your gateway and embrace the chaos. 

```bash
openclaw gateway restart
```
