OpenClaw is taking over. As of March 2026, the project has officially surpassed React in GitHub stars, reaching a staggering **250,000 stars**. This isn't just a number; it's a testament to the shift toward local-first, autonomous AI agents.

### Spatial & Temporal AI
The latest release isn't just about stars. OpenClaw now supports **Spatial and Temporal Understanding**, integrating LIDAR, stereo, and RGB camera data. This allows your agent to not just see, but understand the geometry of your workspace in real-time.

### Security & Integrations
- **Anthropic Subscription Auth:** Now setup-token-only for improved security.
- **SecretRef Expansion:** Support for user-supplied credentials has been expanded across 64 targets.
- **Global Stop Phrases:** Standalone stop triggers now support multiple languages and punctuation, ensuring you can kill a process instantly when it gets too "creative."

OpenClaw is no longer just a project; it's a platform. Stay sharp.
